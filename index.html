how it’s made

ICE CREAM SANDWICH CAKE

Ingredients

  • 6-8 Velvet Ice Cream Sandwiches (Vanilla or Peppermint)
  • 8 oz container whipped topping
  • Fresh fruit (optional)

Instructions

  • Line a loaf pan (8½” x 4½”) with plastic wrap. Leave extra to cover top and sides. Place ½ cup of whipped topping in the bottom of the loaf pan.
  • Cut Velvet Ice Cream Sandwiches to fit the pan and make one layer. Top with ½ cup of whipped topping. Repeat layers until the loaf pan is full.

PEPPERMINT CHOCOLATE DELIGHT

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. hot chocolate
  • ½ cup of Velvet Peppermint Stick Ice Cream
  • 1 candy cane

Instructions

  • Pour hot chocolate into a large mug. Be sure to not fill the mug all the way to the top to allow room for the ice cream.
  • Carefully place ½ cup of Velvet Peppermint Stick ice cream into large mug.
  • Optional Topping: Break up additional candy cane into pieces and use as a garnish.
